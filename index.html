#end.periodpoverty

Did you know that 1 in 3 girls don't have proper access to period products? Our mission at #end.periodpoverty is to take the initiative to bring awareness and help provide resources for women and girls to better access menstrual products.

Definition of Period Poverty

Vision of Impact: Through this website, we hope to serve as an outlet for resources and education on the growing issue of period poverty, where women lack the access to proper hygiene. We want to look beyond the boundaries of period stigma in society, and make it clear that ALL women deserve menstrual products. Developing a supportive community who recognizes how tough periods can be, that products should be free, and connect various organizations to help provide access is our motive.
